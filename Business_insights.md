# Business Insights

## Sales Performance
- Total Revenue generated: $817,860
- Total Orders: 21,350
- Average Order Value: $38.31

## Customer Ordering Trends
- Highest orders were observed on Friday and Saturday evenings.
- Peak ordering hours were between 12 PM – 1 PM and 5 PM – 8 PM.

## Product Insights
- Classic category contributed maximum sales and orders.
- Large-sized pizzas generated the highest revenue contribution.

## Best Sellers
- The Classic Deluxe Pizza was the top-selling pizza.
- Barbecue Chicken Pizza and Hawaiian Pizza also performed strongly.

## Low Performing Products
- The Brie Carre Pizza generated the lowest revenue and orders.
- The Soppressata Pizza showed lower customer demand.

## Recommendations
- Increase promotions during weekend peak hours.
- Focus inventory planning on high-selling pizza categories.
- Consider improving marketing for low-performing pizzas.
